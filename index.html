How Validation Works

For each domain in your CSV, our system performs a comprehensive analysis:

1

Direct fetch first, then Bright Data unblocker for bot-protected sites.

2

Read & Classify
Extracts title, description, and product menu. DeepSeek AI classifies against CPS coverage policy.

3

Return Verdict
Pass/Fail/Mixed with confidence score, matched category, evidence, and reason.

Covered: Powered/durable high-ticket goods (electronics, appliances, tools, firearms). Excluded: Apparel, food, services, consumables.
